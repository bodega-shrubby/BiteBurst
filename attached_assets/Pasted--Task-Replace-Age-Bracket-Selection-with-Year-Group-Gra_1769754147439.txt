## Task: Replace Age Bracket Selection with Year Group/Grade Selection in Onboarding

### Overview
Currently, onboarding asks users to select an age bracket (5-7, 7-11, 11-14). This needs to be replaced with year group selection for UK users and grade selection for US users. The selected year/grade will then map to the appropriate curriculum_id.

### Changes Required

#### 1. Update the Onboarding Flow

**File: client/src/pages/Onboarding.tsx** (or wherever the onboarding steps are defined)

After the user selects their country/curriculum (UK or US), replace the age bracket question with:

**For UK curriculum:**
- Question: "What year group is your child in?"
- Options: Year 2, Year 3, Year 4, Year 5, Year 6, Year 7, Year 8, Year 9
- Each option maps to a curriculum_id:
  - Year 2 → uk-ks1
  - Year 3, 4, 5, 6 → uk-ks2
  - Year 7, 8, 9 → uk-ks3

**For US curriculum:**
- Question: "What grade is your child in?"
- Options: Grade 1, Grade 2, Grade 3, Grade 4, Grade 5, Grade 6, Grade 7, Grade 8
- Each option maps to a curriculum_id:
  - Grade 1, 2 → us-k2
  - Grade 3, 4, 5 → us-35
  - Grade 6, 7, 8 → us-68

#### 2. Update State Management

Store the selected year_group (e.g., "year-2", "grade-3") in addition to the derived curriculum_id.

#### 3. Update User Creation API Call

When creating the user, send:
- `yearGroup`: The selected year/grade (e.g., "year-5", "grade-3")
- `curriculumId`: Derived from the year/grade mapping
- `ageBracket`: Derived from the year/grade mapping (still needed for database)

#### 4. Update User Registration Endpoint

**File: server/routes/auth.ts** (or wherever user registration is handled)

Accept the new `yearGroup` field and store it in the users table.

#### 5. Update Drizzle Schema

**File: shared/schema.ts**


Also update the insertUserSchema to include yearGroup.
